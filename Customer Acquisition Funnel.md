## $${\color{red}Customer \ Acquisition \ Funnel}$$


A customer acquisition funnel is a structured, stage-based framework modeling the journey a prospect takes from first learning about a brand to becoming a paying customer. It acts as a marketing and sales strategy to visualize, measure, and optimize how businesses convert prospects into customers, often focusing on stages like awareness, interest, consideration, and conversion. 

### 🧭 Funnel Overview

```
Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Conversion → Retention → Advocacy
(TOFU)      (MOFU)      (MOFU)          (BOFU)       (Post)      (Growth)
```

---

### 1. 🔝 Awareness (TOFU — Top of Funnel)

**Goal:** Get discovered by the right audience

**Channels**

* SEO (blog, landing pages)
* Social media (organic + paid)
* Ads (Google, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn)
* PR / backlinks
* Influencers

**Tactics**

* Educational content
* Problem-aware content
* Viral / shareable posts

**KPIs**

* Impressions
* Reach
* Website traffic
* CTR

---

### 2. 🤔 Interest (Early MOFU)

**Goal:** Capture attention & start relationship

**Channels**

* Website landing pages
* Lead magnets
* Email signup forms
* Webinars / newsletters

**Tactics**

* Free value (ebooks, checklists, tools)
* Clear value proposition
* Strong CTAs

**KPIs**

* Bounce rate
* Time on site
* Email signups
* Engagement rate

---

### 3. 🧠 Consideration (MOFU)

**Goal:** Build trust & educate

**Channels**

* Email marketing
* Case studies
* Product pages
* Retargeting ads

**Tactics**

* Comparisons
* Testimonials
* Demos / tutorials
* Nurture sequences

**KPIs**

* Email open & click rates
* Returning visitors
* Lead quality
* Demo requests

---

### 4. 💰 Conversion (BOFU — Bottom of Funnel)

**Goal:** Turn leads into customers

**Channels**

* Checkout / sales pages
* Sales calls
* Limited-time offers
* Retargeting

**Tactics**

* Discounts / bonuses
* Urgency (FOMO)
* Risk reduction (guarantees)
* Simplified checkout

**KPIs**

* Conversion rate
* CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
* Revenue
* Cart abandonment rate

---

### 5. 🔁 Retention (Post-Purchase)

**Goal:** Keep customers & increase LTV

**Channels**

* Email / CRM
* Product experience (UX)
* Support
* Loyalty programs

**Tactics**

* Onboarding flows
* Upsells / cross-sells
* регуляр engagement
* Feedback loops

**KPIs**

* Retention rate
* Churn rate
* LTV (Lifetime Value)
* Repeat purchases

---

### 6. 📣 Advocacy (Growth Loop)

**Goal:** Turn customers into promoters

**Channels**

* Referral programs
* Reviews (Google, Trustpilot)
* UGC (user-generated content)
* Community

**Tactics**

* Incentivized referrals
* Social proof
* Affiliate programs

**KPIs**

* NPS (Net Promoter Score)
* Referral rate
* Reviews & ratings
* Virality coefficient

---

### 📊 Core Metrics Formula Cheat Sheet

* **CAC** = Total Marketing Spend / New Customers
* **LTV** = Avg Revenue per User × Lifetime
* **Conversion Rate** = Conversions / Visitors × 100%
* **ROAS** = Revenue / Ad Spend

---

### 🧠 Pro Tips (High-Impact)

* Don’t optimize TOFU if BOFU is broken
* 80% of revenue often comes from retention
* Retargeting = highest ROI channel
* Fast websites = higher conversions (Core Web Vitals matter)
* Messaging > traffic (bad offer kills funnel)

---

### ⚡ Quick Funnel Audit Checklist

* [ ] Clear ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
* [ ] Strong value proposition above the fold
* [ ] One primary CTA per page
* [ ] Lead capture in TOFU/MOFU
* [ ] Email automation in place
* [ ] Retargeting pixels installed
* [ ] Frictionless checkout
* [ ] Post-purchase flow exists
* [ ] Referral or review system enabled
