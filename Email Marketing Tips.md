### 📨 1. SUBJECT LINES (TITLES)

#### 🎯 Goals

* Increase **open rate**
* Spark **curiosity or urgency**
* Communicate **clear value**

#### ✅ Best Practices

* **Length:** 30–60 characters (mobile-first)
* Put **important words first**
* Use **power words** (free, new, now, last chance)
* Personalize when possible: `{FirstName}`
* Avoid spam triggers: ALL CAPS, excessive !!!

---

#### 🔥 High-Converting Formulas

**1. Curiosity**

* “You’re missing this…”
* “This changed everything for us”

**2. Benefit-driven**

* “Get more leads in 7 days”
* “Boost your email CTR today”

**3. Urgency / FOMO**

* “Ends tonight ⏰”
* “Last chance: -40%”

**4. Personalized**

* “{Name}, this is for you”
* “We picked this for you, {Name}”

**5. Numbers / Lists**

* “5 ways to improve conversions”
* “3 mistakes killing your emails”

**6. Question-based**

* “Struggling with low open rates?”
* “Ready to scale your campaigns?”

---

#### 🚫 Avoid

* “FREE!!! CLICK NOW!!!”
* Overly vague: “Important update”
* Too long → gets cut on mobile

---

### ✉️ 2. PREHEADERS (PREVIEW TEXT)

#### 🎯 Goals

* Support subject line
* Add **context + curiosity**
* Increase **open rate**

---

#### ✅ Best Practices

* **Length:** 40–100 characters
* Complement—not repeat—the subject
* Use it as a **second hook**

---

#### 🔥 Effective Patterns

**1. Extend the promise**

* Subject: “Boost your conversions”
* Preheader: “Simple tweaks you can apply today”

**2. Add urgency**

* “Only a few hours left…”

**3. Clarify value**

* “No coding required”

**4. Tease content**

* “Tip #3 might surprise you”

---

#### ⚠️ Common Mistakes

* “View this email in browser”
* Duplicate subject line
* Empty preheader (wasted space)

---

### 🧩 3. EMAIL CONTENT (BODY)

---

#### 🧱 Structure Framework (High-Converting)

**1. Hook (First 1–2 lines)**

* Grab attention immediately
* Reinforce subject line

**2. Context / Problem**

* Show you understand user pain

**3. Value / Solution**

* Present offer, tips, or product

**4. Proof**

* Testimonials, stats, credibility

**5. CTA (Call-To-Action)**

* Clear, visible, action-driven

---

#### ✍️ Copywriting Rules

* Write for **scanners** (short paragraphs)
* Use **bullet points**
* Focus on **benefits > features**
* Keep tone **conversational**
* One email = **one goal**

---

#### 🔥 High-Converting Elements

**CTA Examples**

* “Get started”
* “Download now”
* “Claim your discount”

**Power Words**

* Free, Instant, Proven, Easy, New, Exclusive

**Psychological Triggers**

* Scarcity: “Only 5 left”
* Social proof: “Used by 10,000+ marketers”
* Authority: “Recommended by experts”

---

#### 📐 Ideal Layout

```
[Headline / Hook]

[Short intro]

[Main value / offer]
• Benefit 1
• Benefit 2
• Benefit 3

[CTA Button]

[Optional proof/testimonial]

[Secondary CTA]

[Footer]
```

---

### ⚡ QUICK CHECKLIST

#### Before Sending:

* ✅ Subject line under 60 chars
* ✅ Preheader complements subject
* ✅ Clear CTA above the fold
* ✅ Mobile-friendly layout
* ✅ No spammy wording
* ✅ Personalization works
* ✅ Links tested

---

### 🧠 PRO TIPS (ADVANCED)

* A/B test:

  * Subject lines
  * CTA wording
* Use **preview inbox testing tools**
* Segment audience (behavior, interests)
* Match **email intent with funnel stage**:

  * Awareness → educational
  * Consideration → comparison
  * Conversion → offer-driven
