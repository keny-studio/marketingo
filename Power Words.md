## $${\color{red}Power \ Words \ for \ Marketing}$$

#### Power words are persuasive, high-impact terms used in copywriting, marketing, and communication to trigger psychological or emotional responses. These words aim to grab attention, build trust, spark curiosity, or create urgency, ultimately compelling readers to take action. They transform mundane content into engaging, persuasive messaging.

### ⚙️ 14. POWER WORD FORMULAS

#### Headline Formula

**[Power Word] + [Benefit] + [Time]**

* “Boost conversions in 7 days”

#### CTA Formula

**Verb + Benefit + Speed**

* “Get results instantly”

#### Offer Formula

**Value + Risk Reversal**

* “Try it risk-free for 30 days”

---

### 🚨 PRO TIPS (CRITICAL)

* Don’t overuse → feels spammy
* Match tone to brand (premium ≠ “cheap”)
* Combine 2–3 triggers max per sentence
* Always support power words with **real value**
